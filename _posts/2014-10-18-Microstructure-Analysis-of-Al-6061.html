--- layout: post title: "Microstructure Analysis of Al-6061" author: jsweaver date: 2014-10-18 12:30:00 --- # Microstructure Analysis of Al-6061: Description # *Authors: Jordan Weaver, Ali Khosravani* # Assumptions # ## Salient features ## - Grain size, shape, and distribution (regions of uniform crystal orientation) - Texture (a high degree of probability of specific crystal orientations over random) - Constituent particle size, shape, distribution, and spacing (never were dissolved) - Precipitate size, distribution, and spacing (dissolved and precipitate out of the matrix) ## Data Collection ## - EBSD to measure grain size and texture - Optical and SEM-BSE for constituent particles - SEM-BSE and TEM for precipitates ## Schematic of grains, particles, and precipitates ## Example EBSD Grain Map of Al-6061. Regions of uniform color are individual grains. See this [post](http://alikhosravani.github.io/MIC-Al6061-NIST-project/2014/09/15/Grains-in-OIM-Analysis.html) for how OIM Data Analysis defines grains and other measurements.
